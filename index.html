Graphic Design for Blue Sky Solar Racing

Blue Sky Solar Racing

Blue Sky Solar Racing is a student-run design team at the University of Toronto that builds solar-powered racecars. As Blue Sky Solar Racing is a financially taxing engineering design team, sponsors are vital to the success of the team. As a graphic designer, I helped create appealing marketing and sponsorship materials using InDesign as part of the advancement team. Samples of designed documents are available upon request!

Blue Sky Solar Racing - RBC Proposal

For these mockups, I designed an entirely new racing logo based on existing graphics, and focused on designing iteratively to give the potential client more flexibility of choice. This photo in particular is at an early stage of design, containing four distinct designs and a variety of logo redesigns. These mockups were completed in Adobe Illustrator, while the 3D Render on the previous image was completed using Rhinoceros 6.

Blue Sky Solar Racing - Canada Post Proposal

Within a week, I prepared these two distinct livery designs for a sponsorship proposal package for Canada Post, designed using Adobe Illustrator. I took inspiration from the design of Canada Post delivery vans, as well as traditional livery designs, to show a variety of potential designs should Canada Post sponsor the team.

Bellevue Pavillion (Architectural Design Work)

Bellevue Pavillion

Designed to house artwork in Bellevue Square, Toronto, this project focused on the travesal nature of museums, incorporating it into the walkable nature of the surrounding community of Kensington Market. This render in particular is a Photoshop composite of a photo taken on site and a 3D Rhino model. Completed Fall 2019 individually, as a studio project.

Bellevue Pavillion - Drawings

These scaled drawings were extracted from a 3D Rhino model, and edited using Adobe Illustrator. Open-source greenery and scale figures were included afterwards. They are part of a larger presentation document, available upon request, that was compiled using Adobe InDesign.

Bellevue Pavillion - Grasshopper Script

In designing the Pavillion, I used the visual programming tool Grasshopper to model my design quicker, as it was simple enough to model parametrically. This script shows the general complexity of the project.

Various Architectural Engineering Design Work

Cabin Design - "North Pole"

This wood frame cabin is designed for the dual purpose of productivity and relaxation, and is built as an alternative to working from home. Remotely working in a team, I contibuted key conceptual designs, designed the majority of the wood framing in Rhinoceros 6, and prepared linework references using AutoCAD for all project drawings. Completed Fall 2020 in a group as a studio project.

Desk Design - "Teknik"

This modular desk, designed for architectural engineering students, and intended to be extremely flexible in usage and adaptability. Working with a team remotely, I contributed conceptual designs and prepared linework references using AutoCAD and Rhinoceros 6 for all project drawings. Completed Fall 2020 in a group as a studio project.

Desk Design - Additional Drawings

These additional drawings, modelled in Rhinoceros 6 and drafted in AutoCAD, were designed to add clarity to the flexible functionality our design provided. These drawings, as well as the ones in the previous image, were then later compiled in InDesign by myself as part of a larger presentation package.

About

Hi! I'm currently an undergraduate student studying Architectural Engineering at the University of Waterloo. Previously, I studied architecture at the University of Toronto, where I developed my skills of reading and producing architectural drawings, digital CAD drafting, architectural research and report writing, and graphic design. Some of my interests in architecture include parametric design and fabrication, architectural structural design and sustainable building technologies such as mass timber.

In addition to my studies, I am currently a graphic designer for the student design team Blue Sky Solar Racing, where I design professional business development documents, sponsorship packages and PowerPoint slide decks, and 3D liveries for the car. As a hobby, I also occasionally direct and write films with Minus Comm Films, many of which have won awards in local competitions in the past.

This website contains only a few excerpts of recent projects from the past two years. If you would like full portfolio samples, a resume/CV, or would just like to chat, please feel free to send me an email!
